Strategic Overview

This document outlines the comprehensive training strategy for GROHE in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia for fiscal year 2026. Our goal is to empower our partners with the knowledge and skills necessary to excel, driving both individual success and overall brand growth. This strategy focuses on a blend of in-depth product knowledge, effective sales techniques, and robust support systems, targeting approximately 1,100 to 1,200 individuals over the year.

Key Strategic Objectives:

  • Enhance Product Mastery: Ensure all partners achieve comprehensive product knowledge across the entire GROHE portfolio, including all categories and new innovations such as Euroeco, Cubeo, Showerframe, and HOYB.
  • Accelerate New Product Adoption: Provide targeted, timely training for new product launches to drive rapid understanding and market penetration.
  • Strengthen Partner Engagement: Foster deeper relationships and loyalty with main dealers, other partners, and second-level dealers through valuable and consistent training.
  • Empower Sales & Service Teams: Equip sales and service personnel with the knowledge and skills necessary to effectively represent, sell, and support GROHE products.
  • Drive Performance & Accountability: Implement robust tracking and reporting mechanisms to monitor training participation, effectiveness, and impact on business objectives.

Training Program: Content & Delivery

Core Curriculum

All participants will undergo comprehensive training covering core GROHE product categories and brand values. This ensures a consistent understanding and high level of expertise across all partners.

Module 1: Brand & Bath Category

Includes GROHE brand philosophy, heritage, values, key bathroom collections, innovative technologies, and design principles, and segmentation.

Module 2: Showers & Websites Categories

Focuses on advanced shower systems, SmartControl, Rainshower technologies, and effective utilization of the GROHE website and digital resources.

Module 3: Flushing, Ceramics & Kitchen Solutions

Covers flushing systems, toilets, bidet solutions, ceramic collections, and the full range of kitchen faucets, sinks, and water systems (e.g., GROHE Blue, Red).

Module 4: G4+ & SPA for Projects (New Partners Focus)

In-depth focus on G4+ systems and specific SPA Category tailored for project-based partners and new collaborations.

New Product Focus (Launches starting October 2025)

Dedicated training modules will be provided for all new product launches to ensure our partners are fully equipped to promote and support these innovations.

  • Euroeco: Features, benefits, sales arguments.
  • Cubeo: Design, functionality, market positioning.
  • Showerframe: Unique selling points, application, installation.
  • HOYB: Key features, benefits, and market strategy.
  • Additional Launches (Dec 2025 - Mar 2026): Tailored training as new products are introduced.

Delivery Methodology

In-Person Workshops: Interactive, hands-on sessions in key locations, fostering direct engagement and practical learning. Each group attends 3 core module sessions (1 hour each), plus new product training.

Online Backup Sessions: Monthly virtual sessions for reinforcement, updates, and accessibility for those unable to attend in-person.

Product Launch: Targeted, high-impact sessions immediately following new product introductions.
